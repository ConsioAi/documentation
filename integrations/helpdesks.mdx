Connecting Consio to your helpdesk keeps your support team fully in the loop. When a call ends, Consio pushes the outcome, transcript, and AI-generated summary directly into your helpdesk as a conversation record — matched to the existing customer contact wherever possible. Your agents gain instant context on every call without switching between tools, and your helpdesk becomes a single source of truth for customer interactions across every channel.

## How to Connect a Helpdesk

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations Settings">
    In your Consio dashboard, navigate to **Workspace Settings → Integrations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Platform">
    Find your helpdesk platform in the integrations list and click **Connect**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the Authorization Flow">
    Depending on your platform, you will either be guided through an **OAuth flow** — where you log in and grant Consio access — or asked to **paste an API key** from your helpdesk's settings. Follow the on-screen instructions for your specific platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the Integration">
    Once connected, return to the **Integrations** page and confirm your helpdesk shows a status of **Active**. You are now ready to start syncing call data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Helpdesk integration availability may depend on your Consio plan. If you do not see your platform listed, contact your Consio account manager to confirm availability for your subscription tier.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Before going live, make a test call through Consio and verify that the call record, summary, and outcome appear correctly in your helpdesk. This ensures your contact matching rules and field mappings are working as expected before real customer calls come in.
</Tip>

## How Helpdesk Integrations Work

When you connect a helpdesk platform, Consio automatically handles the following after every call:

- **Call outcome sync** — The call result (e.g. voicemail, connected, transferred) and AI-generated summary are pushed to your helpdesk as a new conversation or appended to an existing one.
- **Contact matching** — Consio uses the caller's phone number or email address to find and match the caller to an existing contact in your helpdesk. If no match is found, Consio creates a new contact record.
- **Call history and context** — Your support agents can see a chronological log of all Consio calls for a given customer, including AI summaries, directly within the helpdesk interface — giving them context before they reply or follow up.

## Klaviyo: Marketing & Segment Sync

In addition to helpdesk platforms, Consio integrates with **Klaviyo** to power campaign contact lists.

When you build a campaign in Consio, you can source your contact list from a Klaviyo segment. Consio syncs the segment membership from Klaviyo and continuously updates the list as customers flow in and out of the segment — so your campaign always reflects the most current audience.

To use Klaviyo for campaign syncing:

1. Connect Klaviyo from **Workspace Settings → Integrations**.
2. In your campaign settings, enter the **Klaviyo Segment ID** you want to use as the contact list.
3. Consio will pull the initial list and keep it updated automatically.

### Campaign Sync Status Codes

After each sync attempt, Consio records a status code on the campaign. Use the table below to understand the current sync state.

| Status Code | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `sync_was_successful` | The segment was synced successfully and the contact list is up to date. |
| `invalid_segment_id` | The segment ID provided does not exist in Klaviyo or is malformed. Update the segment ID in your campaign settings. |
| `inactive_integration` | The Klaviyo integration is disconnected or has been deauthorized. Reconnect Klaviyo from the Integrations page. |
| `segment_missing` | The segment was previously valid but can no longer be found — it may have been deleted in Klaviyo. Select a new segment in your campaign settings. |
| `connection_error` | Consio was unable to reach the Klaviyo API. This is typically a transient network issue; Consio will retry automatically. |
| `unknown` | An unexpected error occurred during sync. Contact Consio support if this status persists. |
