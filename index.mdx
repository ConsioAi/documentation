## How Consio Works

Consio connects your e-commerce store to a powerful calling platform. AI agents handle conversations at scale — following up on abandoned carts, re-engaging lapsed customers, and routing inbound calls intelligently. Every call outcome is tracked and, where a purchase follows, attributed back to the conversation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your store">
    Link your Shopify store (or a supported helpdesk like Gorgias or Zendesk) to your Consio workspace. Consio syncs your customer segments automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure a campaign">
    Create an outbound campaign, assign an AI agent, and choose a customer segment. Set your contact hours and dial-attempt limits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Activate the campaign. Consio dials contacts, the AI agent handles conversations, and call data flows into your dashboard in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pull your data">
    Use the public REST API to retrieve paginated call history and revenue attribution records for reporting and BI integrations.
  </Step>
</Steps>
